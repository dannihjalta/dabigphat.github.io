DANIEL GRAY

Daniel Gray started as an attempt at making something sound like.. something. At first, there was only midi, no audio. So the whole set-up needed to be plugged into a mixer that then was recorded on cassette on the home stereo. Nothing really sounded like what it was trying to sound like, but it did sound like something. "What more can we do?" echoed in the mind. This still hasn't been answered fully, so the quest goes on.
